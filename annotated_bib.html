Choosing My Topic

When looking through the list of topics I could choose from and Black Power is what stuck out to me the most. Only hearing about this topic during Black History Month, I wanted to explore it more. After doing a tiny amount of research on the Black Power Movement before using it as my topic, I found that it fit the theme well, so I chose it. Wanting to learn more about black history (and wanting to get a good grade) is what pushed me to choose my topic.

Researching My Topic

After choosing the topic, the next thing that came was research. For me to finish my project, I needed to conduct good research so, I went to BCPS One for that. I looked through all the online Libraries and found reliable sources. I organized my research into two key parts, one being what the Movement was about and the second being the important figures in the movement. Conducting this research was beneficial to me, because I learned a lot about my topic and a lot about my history.

How Does My Topic Relate to The Theme?

My project topic, Black Power relates to the theme breaking barriers in history. It relates to the theme because this movement helped African-Americans break through racial and social barriers. The Black Power Movement also allowed Black people to fight for social and political equality.
